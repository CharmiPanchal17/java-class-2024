When are Do-while loops used?
Do-while loops are used when the statement is wanted to be executed before the condition 
while while loops are used when the condition is wanted to be executed before the statement.

Differences between while and do-while loops.

1.While loop is entry controlled while Do-While loop is exit controlled.

2.In While loops, the condition is checked first and then the statement is executed. 
And for the Do-while loops, the statement is executed before the condition is checked.

3. In While loops the condition statement is at the beginning while in Do-while loops,
the condition statement is at the end of the loop.
